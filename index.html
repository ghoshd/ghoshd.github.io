Debashis Ghosh is Professor and Chair of the Department of Biostatistics and Informatics at the Colorado School of Public Health. Previously, he was a faculty member in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Michigan and the Department of Statistics at Penn State University. His research interests focus on statistical machine learning, causal modelling and biomarker evaluation with applications in cancer.
